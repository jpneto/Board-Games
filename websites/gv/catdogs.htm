SNORT

Copyright (c) 1970s?, Simon Norton

This game is played on an empty 8x8 board:


  • DROP - On each turn, each player drops one friendly stone into an empty cell not (orthogonally) adjacent to enemy stones.
  • GOAL - Wins the player that makes the last move.

An example

White will win in the next two turns. Black can only play on two cells (the red dots), while White have more available cells.

.

This game is also known as Cats & Dogs (re)invented by Chris Huntoon. There is a ZRF to play Cats & Dogs with Zillions.
