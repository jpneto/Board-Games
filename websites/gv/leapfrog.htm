LEAP FROG

Copyright (c) 2000 Chris Huntoon

This game is played on a 8x8 board with the following setup:
THE FROGS - A frog moves by jumping another frog (of any color) to the opposite empty cell (orthogonal or diagonal).
Enemy frogs jumped over are captured.
GOAL - A player unable to move looses the game.

An example

Red as a winning move which takes 16 jumps! The sequence is: f4:f6-h4::f4-f2:d2-f4:d4-f2-f4-h6:f6-h4-f2-f4-h6:h8 (!) 

After these moves Green only has b1 and d1 becoming  stalemated and so, loosing the game.
