Cavalieri all'Assalto de Re is played on a 8x8 square board with the following setup:
KNIGHTS - The Knights move and capture like the chess Knights (i.e., they jump two cells on any orthogonal direction, then land one cell to the left or right of that jumping direction)
GOAL - Wins the player that places one of his Knights on the last row.
