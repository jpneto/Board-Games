Gem is actually two twin games (Gem-Y and Gem-EE) played on the following 5-side board.

 
  • TURN - On each turn, each player drop one friendly stone in an empty cell.
  • GOAL
    • Gem-Y game: a chain of adjacent stones touching 4 sides, or any side and its opposite 2 sides.
    • Gem-EE game:  a chain of adjacent stones touching 4 sides, or 2 groups touching 3 sides each.
