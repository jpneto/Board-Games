3 MUSKETEERS

Copyright (c) ?? Haar Hoolim

This game is played on the following 5x5 square board:

 
  • MUSKETEER - A musketeer moves to an adjacent (just orthogonal) cell occupied by a soldier (capturing it).
  • SOLDIER - A soldier moves to an adjacent (just orthogonal) empty cell.
  • TURNS - On the first turn the musketeer side moves. Then, turns alternate.
  • GOAL - The musketeers wins if they cannot make a valid move. The soldiers wins if the 3 musketeers are on the same row or column.
 
An example

Soldier's turn. He moves the marked stone to e2. The musketeer must captures d1:c1. Then a1-b1, c1:b1 and the soldiers win, because the 3 musketeers are all on the b column.
