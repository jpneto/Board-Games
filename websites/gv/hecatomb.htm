This game is played on a 8x8 square board with the following setup:

 
SOLDIER - Moves and captures by sliding orthogonally and diagonally thru any number of empty cells until it meets another piece (i.e., like the chess Queen).
KING - Moves and captures to an (orthogonal or diagonal) adjacent cell (i.e., like the chess King).
GOAL - A player wins when it captures the opponent's King.
If there is no capture for 10 turns, the game is a draw.
