RENPAARDEN

Copyright (c) 1981 Fred Horn

This game is played on a 9x9 square board with the following setup:

 
  • TURN - A player must move one of his stones. Each stone moves like the chess Knight except:
    • It can also jump to a cell occupied by a enemy stone and, in that case, it can jump again. This move can be repeated until the stone lands on an empty cell.
  • GOAL - Wins the first player that moves all his stones to the opponent's initial position.
 
An example

White's turn. A possible move would be to move b1 to c3,d5,f4,g6,h8,f9 reaching the last row in just one move. 

Renpaarden means Racehorses in Dutch.
