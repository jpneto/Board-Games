CONNECT 4

The 1st player from the LUDAE project

This applet plays connect 4 (there are a lot of things to clean up. One of them is when you click on the cell, your stone only appears after the computer move).

This player was the best player which evolved from a pool of 20 different players after some 50 generations. There were two different type of players (a) players with neural networks to compute the actual position value, (b) players which assign to every cell zone a different value (the type of the winner).
