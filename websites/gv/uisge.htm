UISGE

Traditional - Ireland

This game (also called the Celtic "Game of the Kings") is played on a 6x7 square board with the following setup:
TURN - On each turn, each player picks a soldier (King) and use it to jump horizontally or vertically over another piece - of the same or different color - and becomes a King (soldier). 
Kings are additionally allowed to move to an adjacent (orthogonal or diagonal) empty cell. After such a move they are not turned again. 
All 12 pieces must be connected horizontally or vertically with each other. Such a connection is broken when two pieces lie diagonally to one another.
GOAL -The player who has only Kings wins.

An example

The green soldier at c5 (the cell with the green dot) jumped to e5 and becomes a King.

Black soldier at e3 cannot jump to e1, because it would disconnect f3 from all the other pieces on the board.
