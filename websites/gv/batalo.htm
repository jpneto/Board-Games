BATALO

Copyright (c) 1992 Harmony Games

Batalo is played on the following board with the following setup: 
BASE - The cell where the King (the marked stone) starts.
SOLDIER - The soldier move to an adjacent empty cell, or jump over friendly pieces or over its own base (stones can continue to jump other pieces, but it is not mandatory).
The soldier cannot move to the base.
The soldier cannot capture enemy pieces.
KING - Move and jump like the soldiers, plus it can slide in a straight line of empty cells. The King may also capture enemy pieces by landing on them.
The King may move or slide over its own Base.
GOAL - Wins the player the moves a piece into the enemy Base.

An example

The white soldier moves to cell [1], creating a fork for the Black King. He must capture the white soldier, and then the White King captures at g7, winning in one move.
