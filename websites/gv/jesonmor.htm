JESON MOR

Traditional - Mongolia

This game is played on a 9x9 board with the following setup:


  • HORSES - Each horse moves like the Chess knight (i.e., one cell on an orthogonal direction then one cell diagonally forward on that direction; it may jump).
  • GOAL - Wins the player that first occupy the center cell and then leaves it.

An example

Blue  moves and wins in 5 moves. There is no way that Green is able to stop the invasion of cell e5. There are 4 blue horses in direct move range, against just 3 green horses.
