Portuguese Progressive Chess

Just another way to progress it!

 

Everybody in the CV world knows Progressive Chess. There is the Scottish, the English and the Italian variant. To my knowledge, this is a new variant of the variant, the Portuguese one. This variant was invented in January 2000.

Portuguese Progressive Chess

  1. The Scottish Progressive Chess rules apply except in the following:
  2. Each piece can only be used once in each turn.
  3. If, by some reason, the player cannot use all its moves, he moves what he can and then pass.
